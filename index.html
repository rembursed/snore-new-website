FAQ

1. Is Snore free to use?

Yes, Snore Bot is completely free to use. However, some advanced features may be available through premium subscriptions in the future.

2. How can I report an issue with Snore?

If you're facing issues with Snore Bot, you can report them by reaching out to our support team through the contact form or by joining our support server.

3. Does Snore collect any personal data?

Snore Bot takes privacy and data protection seriously. We fully comply with GDPR and other relevant regulations to ensure your data is handled with the utmost care. The bot does not collect any personal data. We only store the minimal information necessary for the bot to function properly, and we are committed to respecting your privacy at all times.

4. What happens if Snore stops working?

If Snore Bot stops responding, please try using the !ping command to check its status. If the issue persists, you can reach out to our support team for assistance.

5. What kind of server statistics does Snore provide?

Snore Bot provides real-time server statistics like member count, active users, and server uptime, which can be accessed using the !serverinfo command.
